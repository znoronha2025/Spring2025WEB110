Bringing classic machines back to life — guides, tools, and best practices.

What is arcade restoration?

Arcade restoration is the process of repairing, refurbishing, and preserving coin-operated game machines from the 1970s–1990s. Restorers address electronics, cabinets, controls, artwork, and software to return machines to playable condition.

Why care about preservation?

Preserving arcade machines protects cultural history, provides hands-on learning in electronics and woodworking, and keeps classic gameplay available for future generations.
