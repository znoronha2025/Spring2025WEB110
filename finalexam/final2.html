CRT to LCD Conversion (summary)

Replacing a CRT with a high-quality LCD can improve safety and availability of parts. This section summarizes the process. For a full step-by-step, consult the links in the credits page.

Safety checklist

  1. Disconnect power and discharge CRT before touching chassis.
  2. Work in a well-ventilated area.
  3. Use insulated tools when dealing with mains voltage.
  4. Label connectors when removing them.
  5. Test with a GFCI-protected outlet when restoring power.
